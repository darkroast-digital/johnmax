Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q. What if I would like to adjust the number of teammates I have on my roster?
  • A. When you created your team, you were required to specify the number of teammates you'd like on your team. The application automatically generated the "Fee per Person" amount. At this time, this cannot be modified, so you will simply need to have the initial teammates pay the original fees, and sort the difference out among yourselves. We're making enhancements to the application, to provide more flexibility in the future, however for now, this is the easiest workaround.

  • Q. I'm the Team Captain, can I simply pay the balance myself?
  • A. Yes, as the Team Captain, you can pay the remaining balance of your team, if you prefer to manually collect payment from your teammates yourself. All Team Captain's will be reminded of any outstanding balances, before April 21st's deadline, to ensure payments can be submitted.

    If you are the Team Captain, you can pay your balance by clicking here.
