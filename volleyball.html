Mixed 6's League play Monday through Friday 6:15pm start finishing around 10pm, and Sunday at 3pm, finishing around 6pm.

Social 6's for the average beach volleyball players (Mon, Tues, Wed, Fri and Sun) and Intermediate mixed 6's (Thurs) for the more skilled volleyball players.

League Details

  • 16 teams per night.
  • 4 teams per court playing each of the other 3 teams two times.
  • 17 week season (May 4-August 28).
  • Players must be 19+.
  • Roster of 5 players min and 12 players max.
  • Attendance will be taken periodically to minimize ringers playing in playoffs.
  • A minimum of 2 female players must be on the court for each time, at all times. If you're not using 3, a hole must be used.
  • Team Captain signs team up and pays in full or a deposit.
  • A team can be created and players added later once either deposit or payment is received and team name and team I.D has been given.  A team cannot be a team without a team I.D
  • Additional team members can then enter on their own and enter their information and pay through Pay-Pal.
  • Price is $595 plus HST. $150 non-refundable deposit required to hold team.
  • If paying deposit - full amount is due on April 21/2016.
